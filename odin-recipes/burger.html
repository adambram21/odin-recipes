Perfect Burger

Whats better than a simple burger? This hamburger will make your mouth water and your belly growl for more! Not only delicious but extremely easy to make this is the perfect burger.

  • Ground beef
  • Buns
  • butter
  • Seasonings
  • toppings
  1. Add butter to pan and heat to medium
  2. Mix hamburger meat with seasonings
  3. Form patties with the meat and add to pan
  4. Cook each side for 4 mins
  5. Place on bun add topings enjoy!
