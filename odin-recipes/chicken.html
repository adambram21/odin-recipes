Fried Chicken

A southern favorite, fried chicken recipe! This recipe is quick and easy but full of flavor. Get ready for some finger licking good chicken!

  • Chicken
  • Buttermilk
  • Flour
  • Seasonings
  • Oil
  1. Prepare the flour mixture
  2. Mix seasonings and flour
  3. dip chicken into buttermilk then the flour
  4. heat oil in pan to medium heat
  5. place chicken into the oil cooking on each side for 8 minutes
  6. Let cool and enjoy!
